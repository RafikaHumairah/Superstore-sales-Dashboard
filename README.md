# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built using the **Sample Superstore dataset** in Power BI. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders monitor business performance, identify top-performing products and regions, and understand customer purchasing patterns.

The report consists of three interactive dashboard pages:

- 📈 Sales Performance Dashboard
- 📦 Product Analysis Dashboard
- 👥 Customer & Regional Analysis Dashboard

All dashboards are fully interactive and equipped with slicers that allow users to analyze the data dynamically by:

- Year
- Region
- Category
- Customer Segment

Every KPI and visualization automatically update based on the selected filters, enabling users to perform deeper and more flexible business analysis.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance and trends over time.
- Identify best-selling products, categories, and sub-categories.
- Analyze customer and regional contributions to revenue.
- Support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

# 📈 Dashboard 1 – Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
Provides a comprehensive overview of business performance and sales trends.

### Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Average Sales per Order

### Visualizations
- Sales Trend per Month
- Top 10 States by Revenue
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region

### Key Insights
- Revenue tends to increase significantly during the fourth quarter (Q4), with sales peaking in October.
- California and Texas contribute the largest share of total revenue.
- Furniture is the highest revenue-generating category.
- The West region consistently outperforms other regions.

### Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory and marketing activities before the fourth quarter.
- Focus business expansion on high-performing states.
- Develop targeted strategies to improve sales performance in lower-performing regions.

---

# 📦 Dashboard 2 – Product Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
Analyzes product performance to identify categories and products that contribute the most to revenue.

### Key Metrics
- Total Products
- Total Orders
- Best Selling Category
- Best Selling Sub-Category

### Visualizations
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Sub-Category
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Category and Sub-Category Contribution (Treemap)

### Key Insights
- Furniture contributes nearly half of total revenue.
- Bookcases and Chairs are among the highest-performing sub-categories.
- Revenue is concentrated among a relatively small number of products, indicating a Pareto distribution.
- Office Supplies contribute the smallest portion of revenue.

### Business Recommendations
- Prioritize inventory management for top-selling products.
- Optimize low-performing categories through pricing and promotional strategies.
- Implement product bundling and cross-selling opportunities.
- Focus business resources on products that generate the majority of revenue.

---

# 👥 Dashboard 3 – Customer & Regional Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
Provides insights into customer behavior and geographical sales performance.

### Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Average Sales per Region
- Average Sales per Customer
- Top Region

### Visualizations
- Sales by Region
- Top 10 States by Revenue
- Sales by Segment
- Top 10 Customers by Revenue
- Sales Map

### Key Insights
- The West region contributes the highest share of revenue.
- California is the strongest-performing state.
- The Consumer segment contributes more than half of total sales.
- A relatively small number of customers contribute significantly to total revenue.

### Business Recommendations
- Strengthen customer retention through loyalty programs and personalized promotions.
- Expand marketing initiatives for the Consumer segment.
- Explore opportunities in underperforming regions.
- Develop strategies to retain and grow high-value customers.

---

# 🔍 Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes dynamic slicers that allow users to explore the data from multiple perspectives:

- **Year** – Analyze business performance for a specific period.
- **Region** – Compare performance across geographical regions.
- **Category** – Focus on a particular product category.
- **Customer Segment** – Analyze customer purchasing behavior by segment.

All KPI cards and visualizations respond dynamically to the selected filters, enabling interactive and flexible business analysis.
